Getting Started with ePrescripto

Welcome to ePrescripto! We're here to make managing your prescriptions and healthcare a breeze. Follow these simple steps to get started:

1. Sign Up or Log In:

If you're new here, click 'Sign Up' to create your ePrescripto account. If you're returning, log in with your existing credentials. Don't worry; your information is safe and secure with us.

2. Create Your Profile:

class="step-des"After signing in, take a moment to complete your user profile. This helps us personalize your experience and provide you with the best care.

3. Add Your Prescriptions:

To manage your medications, click 'Add Prescription' or 'Upload Prescription.' Enter the details of your prescriptions, including medication names, dosages, and prescribing doctors.

4. Set Medication Reminders:

Never miss a dose again! Set up medication reminders in your dashboard. Choose your preferred schedule and notification method (email, SMS, or in-app).

5. Explore Your Dashboard:

Your user dashboard is your control center. Here, you can view and manage your prescriptions, track your medication history, and access helpful resources.

6. Add Family Members (Optional):

For family healthcare management, add family members to your account and manage their prescriptions effortlessly.

7. Stay Informed:

Explore our health tips, articles, and resources to stay informed about your medications and overall well-being.

8. Need Help? Contact Us:

If you have any questions or encounter any issues, our support team is here to assist you. Feel free to reach out at [email@email.com] or through our live chat.That's it! You're all set to take charge of your health with ePrescripto. We're here to support you every step of the way.
